# AEGIS-R Laboratory

> **A**nalytics for **E**arthquake and **G**eohazard **I**nfrastructure **S**ystems and **R**esilience

Welcome to the **AEGIS-R Laboratory** at the **University at Buffalo**. We develop analytical, computational, and data-driven methods to improve the understanding of earthquakes and geohazards and enhance the resilience of civil infrastructure.

Our research combines earthquake engineering, geotechnical engineering, seismology, machine learning, remote sensing, and uncertainty quantification to address fundamental challenges in hazard characterization, risk assessment, and infrastructure performance.

## Research Areas

- Seismic Hazard and Risk Modeling
- Surface Fault Rupture Hazard
- Post-earthquake Reconnaissance
- Infrastructure Resilience
